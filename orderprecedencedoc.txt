- Not all syntax included here are "operators" in the strict sense. For example, spread ...
and arrow => are typically not regarded as operators.

- how it looks:
(x) 
- associativity (the direction):
n/a

17: access and call
17a: member access
- how it looks:
x.year
- associativity (the direction):
left-to-right

17b: optional chaining 
- how it looks:
x?.y
- associativity (the direction):
left-to-right
